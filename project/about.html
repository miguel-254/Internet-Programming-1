What we do

We help small-scale farmers achieve a more profitable harvest. We empower them to scale successful agribusinesses, foster sustainable livelihoods, and ensure food security for a growing global population. At Laikipia Avocados & Macadamia, we provide farmers with everything they need to make the most of their land - from seed to sale. This includes financing, training, quality farming inputs, and insurance.
