Michael Waigwa

Founder at Farm Venture Insurance Agency-& Consultancy

About Michael Waigwa

Michael Waigwa is an agricultural insurance expert. He is a specialist in Crop and Livestock Insurance. He has worked mainly with medium to large scale farmers in Narok, Eldoret, Nakuru and Laikipia/Timau. He has been in charge of agricultural insurance departments at ICEA Lion and CIC Insurance Group. He founded Farm Venture Insurance Agency in 2015 . He is good at risk assessment and pricing, underwriting and claims management. He also is a good marketer having developed the business at CIC Insurance and now Farm Venture Insurance Agency

Early Life

Born on 20/10/1982 in Nanyuki. Michael Waigwa spent his early years in a peasant environment in Laikipia,Nanyuki,Umande. Growing up in an agricultural family,he developed a keen interest in general agriculture.

Education

University of Nairobi Bachelor of Science - BS Agriculture, General 2nd Class Upper Division 2001 - 2005 Umande Primary

Achievements

Throughout his career, michael has achieved remarkable milestones which include:

  2. He was In charge of setting up the Agricultural Insurance department -Business Development, Sales, Underwriting and Claims processing at CIC Incurance Company,Kenya
