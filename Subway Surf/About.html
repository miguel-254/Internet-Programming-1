About Us

Welcome to Rocket Carwash, where we're passionate about keeping your car
looking its best. Established in 2003, we've been serving the Havana community with
top-notch carwash and detailing services, driven by our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction. At Rocket Carwash, we understand that your car is more than just a mode of
transportation; it's a reflection of your personality and style. That's why we go above and beyond
to provide meticulous care and attention to detail, ensuring that every vehicle that comes through
our doors leaves looking and feeling refreshed.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide our customers with the highest quality carwash and detailing
services that exceed their expectations. We strive to deliver exceptional results with every wash,
using the latest techniques and eco-friendly products to protect both your car and the environment.

Why Choose Us

Expertise
Our team consists of experienced professionals who are passionate about cars and dedicated to delivering outstanding results. With years of experience in the industry, you can trust us to treat your car with the utmost care and attention
Quality Service
We believe in going the extra mile for our customers. From our friendly staff to our meticulous attention to detail, we're committed to providing a positive and enjoyable experience every time you visit us.

Your time is valuable, which is why we offer fast and convenient carwash services to fit your busy schedule. Whether you're stopping by for a quick wash or treating your car to a full detailing session, we'll have you back on the road in no time.
